Rudolf Costume

Another cute and hilarious Christmas costume idea is this red-nosed reindeer shirt. It’s sure to bring some laughter and cheer from everybody, especially the kids. You’ll be needing a dark brown shirt, and another long sleeved one in a lighter shade. Sew in some white patches and design a comic-looking pair of eyes. Stuff some brown socks for the antlers, and add a red ball for the nose. Hello there, Rudolf!
